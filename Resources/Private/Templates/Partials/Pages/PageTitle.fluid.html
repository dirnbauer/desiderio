The single page-level h1: it carries the page title and is the one real h1 per document. Visible by default; rendered screen-reader-only when the page flags tx_desiderio_h1_sronly. Enable that flag on pages whose first content element (a hero or header) already shows the title — e.g. the home page — so the heading is not duplicated visually. Stands down entirely on a news detail view, where the article supplies its own h1 (newsDetailUid). Expects {page} and {newsDetailUid} (pass arguments="{_all}").
