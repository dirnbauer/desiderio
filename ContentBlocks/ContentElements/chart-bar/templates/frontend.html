The coloured bars are drawn into .chart-bar__canvas by Resources/Public/Js/charts.js (it reads data-chart-data, data-orientation and data-show-grid). That SVG is decorative (aria-hidden): charts.js clears it before drawing, so any markup placed inside it is lost and it carries no accessible name. Everything that conveys the data is server-rendered and stays meaningful without JS and for assistive technology: - a role="img" figure with a concise aria-label + an aria-describedby summary, - a summary stats row (top category, total, lowest and top share), - a visually-hidden (sr-only) data table carrying every category, value, unit and the total.
