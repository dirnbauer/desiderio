The filled trend line is drawn into .chart-area__canvas by Resources/Public/Js/charts.js (it reads data-chart-data and data-fill-opacity). That SVG is decorative (aria-hidden): charts.js clears it before drawing, so any markup placed inside is lost and it carries no accessible name. Everything that conveys the data is server-rendered and stays meaningful without JS and for assistive tech: - a role="img" figure with a concise aria-label + an aria-describedby summary, - a visually-hidden (sr-only) data table carrying every period, its value, the unit and the net change across the range.
